Topic 1: Colors
Red is the color of an apple.
Blue is the color of the sky.
Green is the color of grass.
Yellow is the color of the sun.

Topic 2: Animals
A dog says "woof woof!"
A cat says "meow!"
A cow says "moo!"
A sheep says "baa!"
Animals have different sounds and live in different places.

Topic 3: Shapes
A circle is round like a ball.
A square has four equal sides.
A triangle has three sides.
A rectangle looks like a door.

Topic 4: Numbers
1 apple, 2 bananas, 3 oranges, 4 grapes, and 5 cherries.
Numbers help us count things.
After 5 comes 6, 7, 8, 9, and 10.

Topic 5: The Weather
When the sun is shining, it is hot.
When clouds cover the sky, it might rain.
Rain helps plants grow.
Snow falls when it is very cold.

Topic 6: Good Habits
Brush your teeth in the morning.
Say "please" and "thank you."
Share toys with friends.
Wash your hands before eating.
Sleep early to feel happy and strong!
